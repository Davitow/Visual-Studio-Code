Edinburgh Castle

Location - Castlehill, Edinburgh EH1 2NG, United Kingdom

Edinburgh castle is a historical military fortress 135 meters above sea level, built upon a sturdy rock called castle rock and was once the residence of Scottish kings and queens. While a few parts of the castle continue to remain in military use, as of now the castle is a world renowned visitor attraction.

Camera Obscura and World of Illusions
Location - 549 Castlehill, Edinburgh EH1 2ND, United Kingdom

Camera Obscura and World of Illusions is Edinburgh’s oldest visitor attraction that was established in 1835. Camera Obscura and World of Illusions contains a substantial amount of exhibits at over 100, in addition it includes optical illusions, interactive exhibits and a rooftop camera obscura.

Arthurs Seat

Location - Queens Drive, Edinburgh, Midlothian, EH8 8HG

Arthur’s seat is an extinct ancient volcano that sits 251m above sea level. Arthur’s seat is regarded as the main peak in the group of hills in Edinburgh. Once you make your way up to the peak of the volcano it provides an extensive view of Edinburgh.

The National Museum of Scotland

Location - Chambers Street, Edinburgh, EH1 1JF

The National Museum of Scotland is a significant museum that presents the history of Scotland, nature, art, science, technology as well as cultures from around the world, for example ancient Egyptian culture. Entry into the museum is free and contains over 20,000 exclusive artifacts.
