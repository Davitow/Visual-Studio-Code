Climate

Edinburgh much like the rest of Scotland, experiences a mild maritime climate, meaning that Edinburgh's climate is largely connected to the sea. Edinburgh is also characterised by cold, damp winters and mild summers. Edinburgh's annual average temperature is 48°F and 9°C, with rainfall being common year-round.

Best time to visit

Edinburgh is charming all year-round, but if you would like to avoid the cold or rain, then Summer might be the best time to visit. If you plan to visit during the cooler months, to make the trip more enjoyable for you bring a warm coat, waterproof shoes and and umbrella or waterproof jacket.
