The Royal Mile

Location - Edinburgh EH1 1QS, United Kingdom

The royal mile is a historic central road in Edinburgh that goes through the hub of Old Town in Edinburgh. The Royal Mile stretches from Edinburgh Castle to the Palace of Holyroodhouse.

The Royal Mile consists of medieval buildings, whimsical shops and traditional pubs, a big highlight of The Royal Mile being St. Giles Cathedral.

Calton Hill
Location - 32 Greenside Road, Edinburgh, Edinburgh & The Lothians, EH1 3AJ

Calton Hill is the home of a few renowned Scottish monuments and buildings. These monuments consist of The National Monument, Dugald Stewart Monument, Nelson’s Monument and The City Observatory to mention a few. Calton Hill offers some of the city’s finest views which therefore makes it a popular destination for locals and tourists.

The Scott Monument

Location - East Princes Street Gardens, Edinburgh, City Of Edinburgh, EH2 2EJ

The Scott monument in Edinburgh, Scotland is a monument dedicated to literary figure, Sir Walter Scott who died in 1832. During an architectural competition in 1836, Two years later a design by George Meikle Kemp was approved and construction began in 1840. The scott monument with its striking gothic structure stands 200 feet tall and includes statues portraying characters from Scott’s novels.

The National Museum of Scotland

Location - Chambers Street, Edinburgh, EH1 1JF

The National Museum of Scotland is a significant museum that presents the history of Scotland, nature, art, science, technology as well as cultures from around the world, for example ancient Egyptian culture. Entry into the museum is free and contains over 20,000 exclusive artifacts.
